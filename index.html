The Approach

NO FADS.
NO BULLSHIT.
JUST WORK.

I don't sell 6-pack abs in 30 days. I don't sell "secret" workouts. I've been doing this long enough to know what actually works: consistent training, a nutrition approach that fits your actual life (not the idealized version), and someone who holds you accountable when you want to skip Wednesday's session.

My programming is built around three principles: progressive overload (we get stronger over time, not just the first week), sustainable caloric deficit (you will not be hungry 24/7), and habit stacking (we build the routine before we push intensity). Most people who fail at fitness don't fail because they're lazy — they fail because their program was designed for someone with unlimited time and zero social life.
