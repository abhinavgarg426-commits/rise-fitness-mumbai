About Vikas

THE COACH
BEHIND THE WORK

I played football through school and college in Mumbai. Never seriously enough to go pro, but enough to know what disciplined training looks like. After college I did a certification from the International Sports Sciences Association (ISSA) and started coaching at a gym in Andheri in 2015. In 2016 I moved to my own space — a 1,200 sq ft facility on Main Road, 5 minutes from Andheri Station.

I've trained 500+ clients in 9 years. I've had clients who lost 30kg and kept it off. I've had clients who came in with chronic back pain and left with a functional body. I've also had clients who quit after 3 weeks and asked for a refund — and I gave it to them. I don't hard-sell. If it's not a fit, it's not a fit.

"I don't think I'm special. I think I'm consistent. I show up, I track your numbers, I adjust the plan when it's not working. That's the whole thing."

Certifications: ISSA Certified Personal Trainer, Kettlebell Certified, CPR/AED
