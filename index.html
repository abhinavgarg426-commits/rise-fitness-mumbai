About Vikas

NO FADS. NO BULLSHIT.
JUST WORK.

I've been a personal trainer for 15 years. I started in a small gym in Versova in 2010 with just a bench and a rack. Today I train clients at Gold's Gym Andheri and take a limited number of online clients. I limit my in-person clients to 20 at a time — not because I can't handle more, but because more than 20 means less attention per person.

I don't have a secret workout. I have a system that works: progressive overload, sustainable caloric deficit, and accountability. That's it. Everything else is marketing.

"I don't sell 6-pack abs in 30 days. I sell a structured, accountable training system — and I hold you to it when you want to skip Wednesday's session."

— Vikas Rana, Rise Fitness
